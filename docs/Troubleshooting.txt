Troubleshooting
If your podcast isn't appearing in Google Search or Google Podcasts, try the following steps. Note that Google doesn't guarantee that every podcast will show up in Google Search or Google Podcasts.
Make sure your feed is indexed using the link generator tool. If the tool shows that your feed “is not currently indexed by Google Podcasts,” make sure you are following our podcast guidelines. If you have additional questions, visit the Google Webmaster Central Help Forum.
Register your feed URL with Google
PubSubHubbub site. This can prompt Google to crawl and ingest your feed faster.
